Terms of use

These terms apply to your organisation’s use of Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service.

When using Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service

You must:

  • be authorised to register your organisation for Help to Grow:Digital
  • tell us immediately if you have any security breaches
  • keep your API keys secure
  • get the right levels of consent (to send share and to use data)
  • check that the data you add to Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service is accurate and complies with data protection legislation
  • ensure that any information or other materials provided to be published on the Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service are;
    • complete, true and accurate
    • are in compliance with any Laws and regulations and are not in any way defamatory or offensive.

If you are in breach of these terms, we reserve the right to remove your access to the Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service.

Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service will:

  • keep your data secure (link to privacy notice)
  • give you one month’s notice by email if we change our terms of use or delivery providers
  • publish details of Registered Vendors and Eligible Software Products to SMEs.

Leaving Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service

You can leave Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service at any time. Just contact us (vendors.helptogrow@beis.gov.uk) and we’ll close your account.

When you leave Help to Grow: Digital Vendor Service, all your data will be deleted.
