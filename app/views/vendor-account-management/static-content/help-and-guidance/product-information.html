Help and guidance

Software product information

Once you have received your activation email in the w/c 18th October, created your account on the Vendor Portal, and (if needed) added any additional users you will be able to access the product home page where you will be asked to provide accurate information about each of your listed products.

You will be able to add additional information on your product by clicking on one of your eligible products.

If you click on one of your eligible products, a page like this will follow (as seen if you clicked on AccountingSoftPro):

The information you provide will be used in our Product Comparison Tool to enable SMEs to browse different software options in a comparable form and select the product that best suits their needs.

Product information will be required under the following sections - please only include information for the specific product tier and what is included in the basic software package.

Field Description
Product summary A free text field for you to provide a brief summary of the product. This will be displayed in your product page and product comparison tables on our site, where SMEs can select the software that best suits them. Use this summary to describe: what your software does; who it is aimed at; key business benefits; and any stand-out features.
Product capabilities In your Vendor application form, you provided information about your product’s capabilities against a capability checklist. In this section you should review this checklist and tick/untick the boxes as required, if any updates are necessary. There is an additional free text field where you may describe additional capabilities offered by your product that are not part of the checklist.
Product support & training You should select from the options provided the types of free support offered to SME customers – e.g. email; phone; live chat; FAQs, etc. You should also select the types of training offered to customers – e.g. in person; live online; webinars; documentation; videos.
Deployment platform, compatibility & review details Please select from the options provided the product deployment options and platforms supported –e.g. Vendor cloud (public or private); supported browsers; desktop deployment; server-based/private cloud; mobile applications. We also ask you here to describe how your product integrates with other vendor solutions (e.g. through connectors; open API; ISP/ISV marketplace).
Product review URL Please provide a link to an independent review of your approved product(s) through one of the listed review sites.
Product redemption URL This is a unique link that you will supply via the Vendor Portal by 15th November. This will be used to direct SMEs to the correct product and discount on your website, once they receive their grant token. 
Unique product SKU ID This is a Stock Keeping Unit, to identify products sold on your website. This will be used to distinguish individual products for each Vendor.
Company or product logo (optional) You will have the option to upload a company or product logo to sit alongside your product information and help users identify your product/brand in the product comparison display. A company/product logo is not mandatory.
