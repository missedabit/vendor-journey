Help and guidance

Phase 2: Configuring your API

Once you receive the Swagger documentation, your developer can start to configure the three APIs. Please find key dates below:

  1. w/c 11 October: API specs and Swagger documentation will be sent to you in the Vendor Developer Documentation.
  2. Between 18 October – 5 November: We will hold a roundtable session for all vendors, in which we will present API specifications for the scheme and have a Q&A slot. The session should be attended by the appropriate technical personnel from your organisation.
  3. w/c 18 October: The Vendor Portal will go-live, and you will be required to register your user account. At this point, developer documentation, stub URLs, access code and IP ranges will also be available.
  4. w/c 8 November: Live end-points will be made available by the HtG:D Digital team to start beta testing.
  5. Between 9 – 15 November: You may be selected to join a session with our Digital team for private beta testing.
  6. 15 November: Deadline to submit products ahead of scheme launch.
