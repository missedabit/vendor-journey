Help and guidance

Pricing

We will ask you for your products’ pricing information in the week commencing 1st November as we increase the functionality of the platform. We will ask you to provide information on pricing metrics, free trials and discount periods, contract terms and any additional costs (e.g. set up or installation fees).

You may wish to offer a discount in a different way to that presented on your public facing site, in order to simplify the SME journey or improve billing. In this circumstance, you would need to agree this with BEIS before listing. Any proposal must not result in any additional costs to the SME or BEIS over the 12 months, and it is at the discretion of BEIS whether to accept the proposal.

Calculation of discount

As set out above, you will be required to provide at least the same level of discount to HtG:D customers as to your usual customers. The HtG:D token discount will need to be applied in a consistent manner in order to ensure fairness across vendors.

For wave 1 you should first apply your discount before applying the HtG:D discount. For example, if you offer a 3-month 50% discount period on a product that costs £20 per month (excluding VAT), the split of payment would be:

Month 1 Month 2 Month 3 Month 4
SME £5 £5 £5 £10
Vendor £10 £10 £10 £10
Help to Grow: Digital £5 £5 £5 £10

Note: The grant token will not cover VAT.
